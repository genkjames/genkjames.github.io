Fun Fact The Stopwatch gang was a group of three Canadians who robbed over 140 banks in the US and Canada. During their time of fraud, they were able to steal around 15 million dollars.

But why did the FBI decide to call them the Stopwatch gang? Well, the leader was always spotted wearing a stopwatch around his neck. And since it took around 90 to 120 seconds for them to get in and out of banks, they most likely used that stopwatch to time their robberies.

Fun Fact You might be wondering why your reaction time even matters. Well, studies have shown that having a fast reaction can not only help you move out of a path of danger, but could also be linked with lower mortality. Whereas, people with slower reaction times had a higher mortality rate and were at greater risk of dying from a heart attack.

Fun Fact Did you know that there are 362,800 unique ways of placing Xs and Os on the grid in a game of tic tac toe? Out of those combinations there is a possiblity of 255,168 winning combinations. However, by eliminating all the symmetrical combinations, there are only 138 unique winning combinations.
